Who's ahead? LIKELY REPUBLICAN

No independent polling of the Alme–Bankhead–Bodnar matchup has been released since the June primary. Nonpartisan handicappers rate the race Likely Republican — a February American Pulse poll (taken before Daines withdrew) showed the Republican above 50% in every tested matchup, with the anti-GOP vote split between a Democrat and an independent.

BANKHEAD'S TOP 3
