Their big promises

HICKENLOOPER'S TOP 3
Fix the affordability emergency
Lower drug, housing, and energy costs and hold "the Epstein class" accountable.
Keep public lands public
Helped block the attempted sell-off of Colorado's federal public lands.
Shore up Colorado River water
Federal investment in water supplies and wildfire risk reduction across the West.
BAISLEY'S TOP 3
Rein in federal spending
Cut deficits and regulation he says drive up prices for Colorado families.
Unleash American energy
Expand oil, gas, and nuclear production and speed up permitting.
Back the president's agenda
Runs as a conservative ally of the administration on border and economic policy.
