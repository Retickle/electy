Their big promises

OSSOFF'S TOP 3
Lower health-care costs
Restore the ACA subsidies whose expiry dropped 350,000 Georgians from coverage; keep the $35 insulin cap and Medicare drug negotiation.
Take on corruption
Ban senators from trading stocks and overturn Citizens United to limit anonymous money in politics.
Deliver for Georgia
Keep steering federal money to Georgia farms, infrastructure, and veterans from his Appropriations seat.
COLLINS'S TOP 3
Back Trump's America First agenda
Support the administration's deportation program and border enforcement; he authored the Laken Riley Act.
Cut costs by cutting red tape
Reduce regulations on builders to lower housing costs, and ban corporations from bulk-buying homes.
A trucker's work ethic in Washington
Runs as an outsider businessman; backs term limits, school choice, and lower taxes and spending.
