California's 45th - U.S. House

CA-45 is based in Orange and Los Angeles Counties. Contains the largest Vietnamese population outside Vietnam. This  seat flipped to Democrats by just 653 votes in 2024.
🛈 CA-45 has been affected by Prop 50 redistricting.

Derek Tran

Workers' rights lawyer, Army veteran, and pharmacy owner. Son of Vietnamese refugees.

Who's ahead? LEAN DEMOCRAT

There's no public general-election polling here yet, but two signals favor Tran: he took 53.8% in the June 2 all-party primary (Vo finished second at 14.6% of a fragmented Republican field), and California's Prop 50 redistricting added bluer LA County territory to the district. Still, this seat was decided by 653 votes in 2024 — nobody is treating it as safe.
