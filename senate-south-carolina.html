Their big promises

ANDREWS'S TOP 3
Health care that shows up
Protect Medicaid and rural hospitals; defend childhood vaccine access.
Take on gun violence
Universal background checks and safe-storage laws — her signature issue as a pediatrician.
Lower family costs
Cut drug and childcare prices; end tariffs she says act as a tax on SC families.
GRAHAM'S TOP 3
A strong military
Bigger defense budgets and support for SC's bases, from Fort Jackson to Joint Base Charleston.
Judges & the border
Keep confirming conservative judges; fund the administration's border crackdown.
Deliver for South Carolina
Federal money for the Port of Charleston, I-73, and hurricane recovery.
