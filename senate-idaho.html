RISCH'S TOP 3

Lead on national security

ACHILLES' TOP 3
Break the two-party grip
Join other independents as a "fulcrum caucus" denying either party full control.
Take on monopolies
Challenge corporate consolidation that squeezes Idaho farmers and families.
Rein in the national debt
