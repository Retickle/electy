Who's ahead? SOLID DEMOCRAT

There's no public general-election polling here, and forecasters don't expect a close race. NY-10 is one of the most Democratic districts in the country, so the June 23 primary effectively decided the seat: Lander beat the two-term incumbent by roughly 32 points. He also appears on the Working Families line in November.
