Their stories

DAN OSBORN
1975Born in Omaha; served in the Navy, then became a steamfitter and industrial mechanic.
2021As local union president, led the 77-day strike at the Kellogg's plant in Omaha.
2024Ran for Senate as an independent and came within 7 points of Sen. Deb Fischer — in a state Trump won by 20.
2026Petitioned onto the November ballot with ~12,500 signatures; endorsed by Nebraska Democrats, who ran no candidate of their own.
PETE RICKETTS
1964Born in Nebraska City; rose to COO of the family firm TD Ameritrade; family co-owns the Chicago Cubs.
2014Elected governor with 57% after losing a 2006 Senate bid; re-elected in 2018.
2023Appointed to the Senate when Ben Sasse resigned; won the 2024 special election by 25 points.
2026Won the GOP primary with 82%, seeking his first full six-year term.
